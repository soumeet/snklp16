Rules and Regulation

For selection:

1. There will be two rounds i.e. Elimination round (consisting of riddles and aptitude and the round will be time based) and the final round.
2. The final round will have the qualified members from the elimination round.
3. The problem statement will be provided on spot along with the junk required.
4. The model must be made of specific weight and size mentioned in the problem statement.
5. The judgement will be based purely on the durability and the realistic feature of the model.
6. Decisions made by the judges will be considered final.
7. Maximum team size of two members.
