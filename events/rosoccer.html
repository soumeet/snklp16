General Rules:

1. This is a team event
2. A team may have a maximum of four members
3. Damage to the arena may lead to disqualification of the team
4. No bot is allowed to pick the ball or grip it within its body
5. Decision of the co-ordinators will be deemed final
6. A team can be disqualified on terms of discipline

Bot Specifications:

1. Size of the bot: 25x25x25cm (Â± 5% allowance)
2. Machine should be manually controlled
3. Wire tightening or any such problems will lead to disqualification
4. Weight of the bot should not exceed 4kgs (maximum)
5. Maximum power supply 18V (DC)

Arena Specifications:

1. The arena will be in rectangular shape: 260x130 cm
2. The goal post will be of dimension: 55x60cm
3. Diameter of the ball will be 40mm (Table tennis ball)

Scoring:

1. For First Round:
a. 1000 points will be given at the beginning
b. 50x(number of goals scored) will be added
c. 10x(number of times obstacles are hit) will be subtracted
d. 50x(number of resets taken) will be subtracted
Note: Obstacles dimensions will be displayed on site

2. For Final Round:
a. The team must use the same bot which was used in the first round
b. Two teams will play against each other and the wining team will proceed to next level (knock out stage)
c. Time limit for each match is 5 minutes
d. The fixture will be decided by the co-ordinators
e. The two bots will be placed at opposite ends and the ball will be placed at the centre
f. A six minute time will be given to score goals
g. A three minute half time will be given
h. The team with the maximum number of goals will be the winner
i. In case of mechanical or electrical of the bot, timeout of ten minutes will be given (only once)
j. In case of ball going down the field, it will be given back at the same point where it fell down
Note: All the above rules and specifications may change without prior notice. Decision of event Co-ordinators will be final and binding.
