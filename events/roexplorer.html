Roexplorer

The winner isn't the one with the fastest bot, it's the one who refuses to lose..
Will it be the same with you?? Show your guts and adrenaline at sankalp!

It's time to place your robot in the challenging arena comprising of all possible terrains like sand, pebbles, obstacles, bumpers, bridges, marbles, inclines, sea-saw, dips, cliffs, ramps, banks, etc.

Robot Specifications:

1. The maximum dimension of the robot can be 30 x 30 cm
2. The bots can be powered on-board as well as off-board
3. In case of off-board power supply the wires must be kept long enough to cover the whole track and wire should remain slack during the complete run
4. Max weight must not exceed 5 kg
5. The power supply will be provided maximum up to 18V
6. Readymade toys car are not allowed

Arena:

1. Track width is 40 cm normally and it may change at some place
2. The track surface and course line may have unevenness
3. There will be a inclination, but this will not exceed 30 degrees
4. The design and size of the obstacles in the track which will try to slow down the bot
5. The arena will consist of various obstacle along with sand, bumpers, water, Depth of water etc
